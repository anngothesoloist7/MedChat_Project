Our knowledge base is built on raw medical textbooks, focusing on evidence and recommendations for treating diseases.

1.  **Nelson's Pediatric Antimicrobial Therapy (28th edition)**
    * **Publisher:** American Academy of Pediatrics (AAP).
    * **Authors:** John S. Bradley, MD (Editor in Chief) and John D. Nelson, MD (Emeritus Editor).
    * **Source:** Anna's Archive.
    * **Dimension:** 335+ pages, 18 chapters.
    * **Structure:** Texts and tables.

    * **Source:** Test Y Hoc.
